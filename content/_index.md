### Welcome to Pulse of Life

<img src="https://static.wixstatic.com/media/60af18_c290f019348f49c3b367780c5fe86218~mv2.jpeg" alt="Pulse of Life learning environment" style="width: 100%; max-width: 800px; height: auto; border-radius: 1rem; margin: 1.5rem auto; display: block;">

Pulse of Life is dedicated to providing high-quality, inclusive education to primary school aged Syrian children whose lives have been affected by displacement. Our program offers a nurturing environment where children come to learn, grow their resilience, and build a foundation for a brighter future, regardless of their background or circumstances.
